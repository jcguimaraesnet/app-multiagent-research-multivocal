## Applied Generative AI Course Curriculum

This Applied Generative AI course, designed by industry experts, builds expertise from Python to AI governance.
Understand LLMs, agentic frameworks, and advanced prompting the forces driving the GenAI revolution and gain the
skills to stay competitive in this AI-driven era.

- [Program Induction](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



Kickstart your journey with our Applied Generative Specialization Program, covering all essential concepts in the generative AI domain. This unique Generative AI course is designed to elevate your career, providing comprehensive knowledge and skills to excel in the field.

- [Python Refresher with AI (Optional)](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



Python Refresher with AI is the first step in your AI journey, building the technical foundation for advanced applications. This course introduces core programming concepts, including data types, operators, control structures, functions, loops, and error handling. By understanding these fundamentals, you'll take a crucial step toward tackling other courses in the Applied Generative AI Specialization program.

- [AI Literacy](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



AI Literacy bridges the gap between Python skills and real-world AI applications, enabling you to assess AI feasibility and optimize AI-generated content. This course explores AI, machine learning, deep learning, and key breakthroughs shaping industries. While addressing AI security and ethics, you’ll dive into transformers, NLP, generative AI, and open-source tools like Hugging Face. Hands-on learning with GPT models will help you apply AI to business and creative contexts.

- [Advanced Generative AI: Models and Architecture](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



This course deepens your understanding of modern AI systems by exploring generative models and transformer-based architectures. This course covers VAEs, GANs, LLMs, and attention mechanisms, equipping you to design, fine-tune, and optimize AI models. With hands-on learning in LangChain and AI workflows, you'll gain the skills needed to develop scalable GenAI applications and assess their future impact across industries.

- [Advanced Generative AI: Building LLM Applications](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



This course focuses on the hands-on implementation of LLM-driven solutions. This course covers advanced prompt engineering, LangChain frameworks, and fine-tuning techniques to create high-performance AI applications. You’ll build and deploy retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems, customize LLMs for domain-specific tasks, and evaluate model performance, equipping you with the skills to develop optimized, real-world AI solutions.

- [Advanced Generative AI: Agentic AI Frameworks With Model Context and Tooling Protocols](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



Discover the core principles of Agentic AI, covering essential design components such as perception, cognition, and action. Create advanced agent workflows with LangGraph, AutoGen, and CrewAI. Utilize the MCP protocol for effortless tool connectivity and interaction. Learn how enterprise adoption, governance, and security influence the evolution of agentic systems.

- [Advanced Generative AI: Image Generation](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



This course takes AI automation to the next level, enabling agents to generate and refine high-quality images. Leverage stable diffusion, denoising, and autoencoders to create visuals from text. Explore shared embedding spaces to enhance multimodal AI and develop custom AI-generated images for diverse applications. Expand your AI’s creative potential with practical techniques and real-world use cases.

- [Generative AI Governance](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



As AI-generated content advances, responsible governance is essential for ethical, secure, and transparent AI systems. This course explores governance challenges, regulatory considerations, and best practices for deploying generative AI. Learn to mitigate risks, address bias and privacy concerns, and navigate legal compliance. Develop frameworks to ensure fairness, accountability, and trust in AI-driven industries.

- [Capstone Project](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#)



In this final step, apply your skills to design, train, and deploy a functional generative AI solution. Address ethical concerns, bias mitigation, and regulatory compliance while ensuring technical excellence. This project reinforces real-world implementation, preparing you to create ethical, secure, and impactful AI solutions for business and society.

- ### What is GenAI?

Generative AI (GenAI) is artificial intelligence that creates new content—such as text, images, code, and music—by learning patterns from vast data sets. It uses models like ChatGPT and Stable Diffusion to generate human-like responses, artwork, or software code. GenAI is transforming industries by automating creativity, enhancing productivity, and enabling personalized experiences. This applied GenAI course provides hands-on training to harness this powerful technology.

- ### AI vs. GenAI: What's the difference?



[arrow](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#ai-vs.-genai:-what's-the-difference)[arrow](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#ai-vs.-genai:-what's-the-difference)









AI (Artificial Intelligence) refers to systems that mimic human intelligence to perform tasks like decision-making, problem-solving, and data analysis. It includes rule-based automation and machine learning. Generative AI (GenAI) is a subset of AI that creates new content—text, images, or code—by learning patterns from data. While AI optimizes tasks, GenAI focuses on generating original outputs, enhancing creativity and automation.

- ### What is the main goal of generative AI?



[arrow](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#what-is-the-main-goal-of-generative-ai)[arrow](https://bootcamp.engin.umich.edu/certifications/applied-gen-ai-course#what-is-the-main-goal-of-generative-ai)









The main goal of generative AI is to create new content—such as text, images, music, or code—by learning patterns from data. It enhances creativity, automates content generation, and personalizes user experiences. Unlike traditional AI, which analyzes and optimizes tasks, generative AI focuses on producing original outputs. This applied GenAI course will help you learn to deploy GenAI tools and techniques in real-world cases.
