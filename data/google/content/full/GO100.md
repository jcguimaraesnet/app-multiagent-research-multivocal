# **What is Gen AI Skillup?**

Accelerate your Gen AI skills with expert led sessions, guided learning paths  and hands-on training from introductory to advanced levels. Upon completion, you'll earn skill badges and a program completion certificate to showcase your expertise.

Accelerate your Gen AI skills with our comprehensive program featuring expert led sessions, guided learning paths and hands-on training. Whether you're a beginner looking to grasp the basics or an experienced professional aiming to stay ahead of the curve, our program will equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of generative AI. Upon completion, you'll earn skill badges and a program completion certificate to showcase your expertise.
